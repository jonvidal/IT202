About

In trending event, fantasy sport games has exploded, where sport fans bet and gamble their money among friends and other users. Corporation like Yahoo!, ESPN, Fanduel, and Draftkings offers a platform for daily and league fantasy sport. Before, it began as a fun game played with group of friends, but today it is a multi-billion dollar that has a tremendous influence on real and live sports.
